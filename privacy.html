Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 13, 2026  ·  Frank Cover / Gavin Dyer

Frank Cover is operated by Gavin Dyer, a licensed independent insurance advisor in Alberta. This policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights under Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and Alberta's Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA).

01 — Who We Are

Frank Cover is a trade name operated by Gavin Dyer, Independent Life & Disability Insurance Advisor, licensed in Alberta under the Insurance Act (RSA 2000, c I-3). AIC Life Licence M-124004-SP-2025 · A&S Licence Q-124004-SP-2025.

Our principal place of business is Calgary, Alberta, Canada. You can reach us at:

  • Phone: (403) 536-6897
  • Website: frankcover.ca

02 — What We Collect

When you submit the contact form on this website, we collect:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your phone number
  • The type of coverage you are exploring
  • Your preferred coverage amount range
  • Your preferred time to be contacted

We also collect standard technical information automatically when you visit this site, including your IP address, browser type, referring URL, pages visited, and time of visit. This is collected through Google Analytics and is used in aggregate form only.

We do not collect payment information, health information, or government identification numbers through this website. Any such information required for an insurance application is collected separately and directly through the applicable carrier's application process.

03 — Why We Collect It

We collect your contact information solely to:

  • Contact you to discuss your insurance needs as requested
  • Provide you with a free, no-obligation insurance review
  • Run comparative quotes across our carrier network on your behalf
  • Follow up on any application you choose to proceed with

We do not collect your information to sell it, rent it, or use it for any purpose other than responding to your direct request for insurance information.

04 — How We Use Your Information

Your contact information is used only to reach you regarding your inquiry. We will not add you to a marketing list, send unsolicited emails, or share your information with any party other than as described in Section 05 below.

If you choose to proceed with an insurance application, your information will be shared with the applicable insurance carrier as part of the application process. That carrier's own privacy policy will govern the use of your information from that point forward.

05 — Third Parties We Use

We use the following third-party services in operating this website:

  • Web3Forms — processes form submissions and delivers them to our email inbox. Your form data passes through Web3Forms' servers. Their privacy policy is available at web3forms.com.
  • Google Analytics — collects anonymized website usage data to help us understand how visitors interact with the site. No personally identifiable information is shared with Google Analytics. You can opt out at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.
  • Google Ads — we may use Google Ads to advertise this service. Remarketing is not currently enabled. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to any other outside party.

06 — Data Retention

If you submit a form and do not proceed with any insurance application, we retain your contact information for no longer than 12 months, after which it is deleted from our records.

If you proceed with an insurance application, records are retained in accordance with our obligations under Alberta's insurance regulations and the applicable carrier's record-keeping requirements.

07 — Your Rights

Under PIPEDA and Alberta's PIPA, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request a copy of your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Withdraw your consent to our use of your information at any time
  • Request deletion of your personal information, subject to our legal retention obligations

To exercise any of these rights, contact us by phone at (403) 536-6897. We will respond within 30 days.

08 — Cookies

This website uses cookies for Google Analytics purposes only. These cookies do not contain personally identifiable information. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without affecting your ability to use this website.

09 — Security

This website is served over HTTPS. Form submissions are transmitted via an encrypted connection. We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10 — Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, the effective date at the top of this page will be updated. Continued use of this website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11 — Contact & Complaints

If you have questions about this policy or a complaint about how we've handled your personal information, contact us at (403) 536-6897.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may contact the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada at priv.gc.ca or the Office of the Information and Privacy Commissioner of Alberta at oipc.ab.ca.
